Ed Cheverton

ILLUSTRATION CAN BE A POWERFUL TOOL FOR COMMUNICATION


Ed Cheverton is a Bristol based Artist and Illustrator. His work features collage, comics, zines and toys! This combination of multimedia practice and fun, color-filled content has led him to work with multiple publications, including WIRED UK, and Cheverton has recently branched out into film.

Try to draw yourself in Cheverton’s style! Look around your room, what shapes do you see? Can those shapes be the same as the ones in your face? Keep drawing shapes! Draw yourself as simply as you can, or if you don’t want to draw yourself draw anyone, or no one! Keep drawing.
