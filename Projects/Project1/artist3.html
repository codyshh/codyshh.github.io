Jessica Hische

TYPE CREATES THE WORLD OUR WORDS LIVE IN.


Jessica Hische is an illustrator, hand-letterer, and type designer. She's worked on everything from postage stamps, to children's books, to designing whole typefaces for a single film. Her work is full of fun and whimsy, and it's hard not to smile looking at her letters.

Pick a word! Write it down on a piece of paper. Stare at it! What does the word mean? How can you communicate that meaning with type? Think about phoenetics, think about symbolism, think about how the word exists in the world. How will people read the words you write?
