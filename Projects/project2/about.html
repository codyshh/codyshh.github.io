A little about us...

House of Vintage is a Pacific Northwest vintage store with locations in Portland, OR and Vancouver, WA. Each store is comprised of a collective of vintage dealers. Having multiple dealers allows us to offer a wide variety of vintage finds. Every day there are over 70 different experienced vendors (between our two locations) out there picking amazing finds for you. Each of our locations has a speciality focus plus a variety of additional vintage treasures. Our Portland location specializes in vintage fashion. At our Portland store you will find rows of vintage clothing ranging from the 1920s through the 2000s. In addition, our Portland store offers some vintage housewares, jewelry, pop culture collectables, art and furniture. Our Vancouver location specializes in mid-century furniture, decor & collectables. In addition, our Vancouver store offers vintage and antique furniture, art, housewares, jewelry and clothing.
